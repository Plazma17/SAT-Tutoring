How It Works
Step-by-step process for students and parents to get started and progress smoothly.
  1. Contact us by call or text to express interest and schedule a free consultation.
  2. Bring or send your College Board score report (practice or real SAT) before the consultation.
  3. Our tutors collaborate to analyze your results and recommend the best course to reach your target score.
  4. We create a personalized study plan and estimate how many sessions and hours you need.
  5. We share availability from both tutors and you choose your preferred times.
  6. Sessions begin via Zoom or in person with a focused lesson plan addressing your weakest areas.
  7. Future sessions are scheduled based on progress and the plan set at consultation.

This method ensures a clear path from initial testing to test day improvement while tailoring pacing and content to each student.
